Capabilities

Why KeyR?

Everything you need to automate repetitive tasks on Windows.

⏺

One-Click Recording

Hit record and capture every mouse movement, click, and keyboard press. No configuration needed.

▶

Instant Playback

Replay your recorded macros instantly. Loop continuously or run a specific number of times.

⚡

Speed Control

Adjust playback speed with a precise multiplier. Speed up tedious tasks or slow down for accuracy.

⌨

Custom Hotkeys

Set your own global hotkeys for recording and playback. Trigger macros without switching windows.

🔒

Save & Load Macros

Save your recordings as encrypted .tt2 files. Share them or reload them anytime you need.

◑

Dark UI

A clean, modern interface with always-on-top mode. Stays accessible without getting in the way.
